Scope of Work: Post-Construction Final Clean

Task Inclusions Exclusions
Debris, Dust & Plastics Removal Sweep/vacuum large debris, dust all surfaces (walls, ceilings, fixtures), remove protective plastics/tapes, collect and bag construction waste for client-provided skip bin. Does not include skip bin hire. Debris removal is limited by what can be safely carried (5kg per cleaner).
Windows, Tracks & Seals Clean internal-only glass up to 2.5m. Includes vacuuming tracks and wiping frames. Includes up to 244 window panels (10% of mÂ²). Excludes exterior cleaning, heights above 2.5m, and removal of paint or concrete splatter.
Walls & Fixtures Dust walls top to bottom, spot clean minor marks/scuffs, wipe down switch plates, outlets, light fixtures, vents, and built-in joinery. Excludes major wall repairs, repainting, or washing entire walls. Cannot remove deep stains or graffiti.
Kitchens & Bathrooms Deep clean and sanitize sinks, countertops, toilets, showers/tubs; clean inside/outside of appliances; polish mirrors and fixtures. Excludes cleaning inside heavily soiled appliances, descaling, or significant mold remediation.
Floors Sweep/vacuum all floor types, mop hard floors, spot-scrub tile/grout, clean baseboards, and thoroughly vacuum all carpets. Excludes removal of deep-set stains (e.g., oil, paint), floor polishing/buffing, or professional carpet deep cleaning (available as an extra).
